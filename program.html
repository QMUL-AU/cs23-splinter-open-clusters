Program

Note: A complete and publicly accessible program will be posted online by May 1st, 2026.

Session Format

The splinter session will consist of:

  • One invited review talk (20+3 minutes each)

    • Invited Talk: Review on Photometric and Spectroscopic Observations of Open Clusters.

  • Contributed talks (10+2 minutes each, 8-9 talks selected from submissions)
  • 2-minute Research Highlights/Poster Pop-ups (4-6 quick-presentations with 2-3 slides)
  • Panel Discussion (15-20 minutes) - Summary of key insights and open exchange among all participants
